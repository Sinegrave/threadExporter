Basics
  • Threads can be added via the 'Add to Tracker' displayed on the thread itself. Ideally, the first comment made by your journal should be the one tracked.

  • It's recommended that longer threads [20+ comments] be saved near the top of the thread rather than the bottom. It's much faster.

  • Threads will be deleted when uninstalling/reinstalling, so it's recommended that all threads be exported prior to changing versions.
